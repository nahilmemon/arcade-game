Instructions

Story

One day, Luka and her friends were bored and hungry, so they decided to go fishing. As they race over to the docks, a huge gust of wind preceding a stampede of humongous lady bugs sweeps over and they lose their fishing gear during all the madness. Help Luka and her friends find their fishing gear and get to the docks without getting stomped on.

How to Play

Use the , , , and keys to move around.
Collect the fishing rod before heading over to the docks.
Only one friend can stand in one spot on the dock.
If you get hit by a lady bug, you'll lose a life.
Good luck!

You won!

Congratulations! You managed to help Luka and her friends find all their fishing gear and get to the docks in one piece. Thanks to you, they had a blast and caught enough fish to have a grand BBQ.
